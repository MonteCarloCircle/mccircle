About This Website

Welcome to the official website of MonteCarloCircle, the first international reading group about Monte Carlo methods. Founded in 2024 by three passionate PhD students, MCCircle is a community dedicated to advancing our understanding of Monte Carlo methods.

Our mission is simple: knowledge enhancement and community building. The idea of this group is to provide a platform for exploring cutting-edge research and the latest advancements in the statistic field, with a special focus on Monte Carlo methdos.

We invite statisticians and researchers to join us and share their insights and findings, fostering a vibrant academic exchange through regular virtual meetings, resource sharing, and engaging discussions.

How to Join

Who are the Organizers

Noemi is a PhD candidate in statistics at the University of Padova. She has been a visiting student at University College Dublin for nine months. Her research interests lie on density-based clustering methods for complex data such as categorical, mixed and high-dimensional network data.

Lapo is a seasoned statistician with over a decade of experience in the field. She specializes in Monte Carlo methods and their applications in data science.

Daniele is currently a PhD student at University of Perugia and a visiting PhD student at University College Dublin. He holds a master's degree in Statistics and Actuarial Science. His research interests lie in Bayesian inference and computational statistics, with a focus on latent variable models and Monte Carlo methods.
